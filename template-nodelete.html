ABOUT US

TANGIBLE is founded by the C-suite executives overseeing architecture and construction departments of Full Power, a public company listed on Ho Chi Minh City Stock Exchange back in 2009. Mike Chang is a licensed structure engineer and Kent Liu an architect. Jason Lai, the Business Development and Key Account Sales Manager of Caterpillar/V-TRAC for the country of Vietnam joins the company at a later date bringing with him over 30 years of experiences in finance, operations, business development and consulting.

Our Founders

Construction Project Management

When millions are on the line in a construction project, avoiding costly mistakes becomes priority number one. Bound by strict time parameters and financial constraints, every detail and contingency must be accounted for. 

TANGIBLE helps our clients do just this. From planning and design, to preconstruction, budget allocation, resource management, procurement, construction, and more, helping you keep the entire construction process efficient and on track. Even the most thorough plans can’t anticipate every hiccup along the way, we regularly monitor and evaluate the progresses, which is vital for staying on course. In managing changes, we adapt as needed while remaining within the parameters of the project’s plan.

With an extensive understanding in terms of legal regulations, wide practical experience, and long-lasting relationships with the administrative authorities across, we are proud of providing support for domestic and overseas investors throughout their implementation process of investment projects in different business. Not only we provide the construction project management services but also the investment consulting to help our clients meet all the regulatory requirements and obtain all needed license. This integrated one-stop service proves to be highly efficient and effectively minimize risks and guarantee the success of our clients in their investment projects in Vietnam.

TANGIBLE exists to help our clients turn their construction plans into reality. It goes without saying that our clients are our number one focus; we love solving their problems and delivering their projects. What we love even more is to delight our clients by exceeding their expectations.

TANGIBLE does not exploit the over-promised, under-delivered, or under-quote counter-productive practices in order to secure contracts. The reason that our clients are happy with us and to recommend us is not necessarily that we quote the lowest prices and the shortest delivery times but we can be relied on to deliver what is promised. It is our uncompromised mission to be proud of the work that we do and never to be associated with the fraud and sharp practice. We strive to be a shining trusted brand of what a construction project management company should be.
