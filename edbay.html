EDBAY

Communal E-Learning Portal

Scroll down to content

Promoting ICT-Supported Collaborative Teaching

EDBAY is the combination of the two rhyming words from Vietnamese. The abbreviation for education “ED” is from “dy”, meaning teach/educate; and “BAY” is from bi (hc), meaning lessons.

The COVID-19 pandemic has brought an impetus for the digital transformation and changed education forever. Inspired by the proposition from Prof. Huynh Thanh Tien, Director of Resources Center for Community (RCCD) at Vietnam National University HCMC An Giang University to make courses available on the Canvas, a Learning Management System (LMS) that has been adopted by all of the schools of Harvard University.

One of the meanings for the word bay is “any of various compartments or sections used for a special purpose”, e.g., cargo bay. The setup of the EDBAY.ORG website is the augmentation of Dr. Tien’s vision and in hope to develop it into a communal endeavor to serve as a portal of collecting the lessons made available online from the peer lecturers for the purpose of facilitating students learning.
